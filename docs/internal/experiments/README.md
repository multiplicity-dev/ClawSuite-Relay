# Internal Experiments

This folder is for structured experiment capture that may influence ClawSuite Relay design, defaults,
documentation, or interpretation.

The goal is not formal lab rigor.
The goal is to stop losing important experimental work inside anecdote and memory.

Use this folder when:

- something is intentionally varied
- the result may matter later
- the result may influence product direction, interpretation, or both

Each experiment should get:

- its own note
- a clear question
- named variants or conditions
- outcome measures
- run-level observations
- a short interpretation
- a separate product-implication section

This keeps:

- what happened
- what it might mean
- what should change

from collapsing into one impressionistic paragraph.
